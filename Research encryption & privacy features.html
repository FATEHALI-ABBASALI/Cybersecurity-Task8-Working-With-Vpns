1 🔐 Proton VPN Encryption and Privacy Features

1.1 🔒 Strong Encryption

1 Proton VPN uses AES256 encryption to protect traffic end to end.

2 Perfect Forward Secrecy ensures session keys are rotated to protect past sessions.

1.2 ⚙️ VPN Protocols

1 WireGuard UDP is visible in the screenshot and offers a modern lightweight protocol.

2 Other supported protocols include OpenVPN and IKEv2 IPSec for compatibility.

1.3 🛡️ Kill Switch

1 Kill Switch blocks all internet traffic if the VPN disconnects to prevent leaks.

1.4 🧯 NetShield

1 NetShield provides DNS filtering to block ads trackers and malware domains.

1.5 📝 No Logs Policy

1 Proton VPN enforces a no logs policy under Swiss privacy law and does not store browsing activity.

1.6 🔐 Leak Protection

1 DNS and IP leak protection routes DNS queries via encrypted Proton servers to avoid exposure.

1.7 🔁 Secure Core

1 Secure Core uses multi hop routing through privacy friendly countries to reduce risk of traffic correlation.

1.8 🧩 Split Tunneling

1 Split Tunneling lets users choose which apps or sites use the VPN and which use the normal connection.

1.9 🔀 Port Forwarding

1 Port Forwarding helps advanced users improve connectivity for P2P while maintaining encryption.
